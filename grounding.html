Grounding Mediation

Materials

  • A space to sit or lay down
  • Yourself

The Meditation

Sit or lay down comfortably and close your eyes. Imagine roots coming down from your body into the ground below you. They may be small tendrils the first time you sit, but they will eventually grow as you continue to do this practice. See your roots grow and spread around you. Everything your roots touch is sacred. Sit with this feeling for as long as you like.

When you are ready bring your roots back in. You are of this place. You are part of this place. The earth supports you and you feed back into it. Your roots are always there for you. Open your eyes and gently return to your day.
