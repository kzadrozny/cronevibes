This tea should not be consumed by children, people who are pregnant or breastfeeding, or for the treatment of any serious medical condition. More detailed concerns are broken down by herb below.

Herbs Used

Skullcap

Benefits
Helps relieve insomnia and anxiety, calming and great for menstrual cramp relief. Bitter and vegetal flavor.

Warnings
Do not consume if on blood thinners, cholesterol medication, or pain killers.
Not to be consumed by those with impaired liver function.
Not for those who are pregnant or breastfeeding.

Marshmallow

Benefits
Restores endocrine system, cooling nervine. Soothes inflammation. Sweet and cooling flavor.

Warnings
Interacts with blood sugar levels and should not be consumed by those with diabetes.

Blue Vervain

Benefits
Non-sedating nervine, used for anxiety and for feeling overwhelmed. Lowers blood pressure, helps lymph flow, good for hot flashes, hormone balancer. Very bitter flavor.

Warnings
Not be consumed by those who are pregnant, breast feeding, or with impaired liver or kidney function.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 parts dried skullcap
  • 2 parts dried marshmallow
  • 1 part dried blue vervain

Making & Using

Blend dried herbs together and store in an airtight container. To make tea, pour boiling water over herbs and let steep for 3-5 minutes.
