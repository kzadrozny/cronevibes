About

Kathy Zadrozny (she/her), CYT 200, is a folk practitioner drawing from her Slavic, Italian, and Venezuelan heritage. Her practice leans toward simple nature-based movements and rituals that involve few items. Kathy's sessions meet your energy where you are in the hopes to shift and transform where you want to be.

Kathy finds strength in ancestor work, candles, tarot, herbal oils, and teas. She shares her practices and rituals through newsletters, zines, and YouTube as tools to help you find ease in your strength.
